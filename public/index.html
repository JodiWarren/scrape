About us

We believe in connected devices

Vanamco stands for vanilla ambient computing. That's computing applications and services that are built to work out of the box, use at your home and on the road, and provide you with the information you need in any context.

We build fast and rock-solid

With technology changing at the rate of our heartbeat, long development timeframes and conceptual phases just don't sound right. We like to build software with state-of-the-art technology, and at a pace that lets us get ahead of the game.

And we're always looking for talent.

You build fancy instead of reinventing the wheel? You're into networked JavaScript and native extensions? You're building your awesome designs directly in HTML and CSS? If that sounds about right, be sure to get in touch!

Our expertise & skills

Information Architecture

Using our experience we develop information architecture that finds the most efficient way of delivering the solution that you are looking for.

Responsive Design

To make sure none of your users are left behind, we design responsively. This means that our web interfaces do not only look good, but equally work on desktops, laptops, mobiles and tablets alike.

Web Engineering

From Cloud-based backends and services, to fast and intuitive web interfaces in standardized HTML5, CSS and JavaScript - we build online, and love doing so.

Customer Satisfaction

All the effort would be in vain if at the end, the customer wouldn't get what they wanted. In all our work, customer satisfaction is the key element to successfully building solutions - please, let us show you.
